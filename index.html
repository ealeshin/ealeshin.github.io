About me
I am 31 years old programmer from Russia. I am a backend web developer at M-Social Production studio and also code web apps frontend. Perl was my first programming language in 2005. I used it for developing a Twitter bot for my cat. I am an engineer by education. Before I started at commercial web development I had projected radio masts and towers, 3G/4G base stations, their power supply lines, electrical grounding and lightning protection. Also I made radiofrequency radiation reports for people safety. But my passion for programming finally won. Currently I code mostly on PHP, SQL and JavaScript. I play the piano and the guitar. I wear a black coat, drink a lot of coffee and love autumn more than any other season.
