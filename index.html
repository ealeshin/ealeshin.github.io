About me
I am 31 years old programmer from Russia. I am a backend web developer at M-Social Production studio and also code web apps frontend. How did it all begin? When I was a schoolboy I was digging in Linux a lot and liked writing shell scripts to automate everything. Perl was my first programming language in 2005. Later I used it for developing a Twitter bot for my cat. But even then this language was not popular and I preferred PHP. I don't remember how I learned HTML and CSS. Maybe I've known it since I was born. I am an engineer by education. Before I started at commercial web development I had projected radio masts and towers, 3G/4G base stations and made radiofrequency radiation reports for people safety. But my passion for programming finally won. Currently I code mostly in PHP, SQL and JavaScript, configure servers and work with databases. I have many other interests. I was a swimmer, a concert photographer, a punk rock band guitarist. I was cast in a movie. Now I play the piano in my free time. I wear a black coat, drink a lot of coffee and love autumn more than any other season.
